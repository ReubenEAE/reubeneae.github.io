Powerlifting

Powerlifting is a sport where athletes perform 3 different lifts and compete for the bigger total ( weight of lifts summed ).

It started out with my friends introducing me to the gym. I learnt about powerlifting and started to grow a passion for it. It keeps me physically fit and pushes me to constantly overcome and define the limits of what I am able to achieve physically and mentally.

Rock Climbing

Scaling Rocks ( Again )

I used to be deathly terrified of heights.
I often go rock climbing with friends. I have come to terms with my height phobia by using this opportunities to get used to it. Needless to say, I now thoroughly enjoy looking forward to new routes to climb and new things to explore.
